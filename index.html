Pittsburgh

Pittsburgh, often referred to as the "Steel City," is a vibrant and culturally rich metropolis nestled in western Pennsylvania, celebrated for its scenic riverfront setting, world-class museums. We visited the tram which gave us a beautiful view of the city, and while walking around the city we saw the city courthouse that was built in the 1800s.

Cedar Point

Cedar Point, located in Sandusky, Ohio, is a world-renowned amusement park that consistently thrills visitors with its incredible roller coasters and diverse attractions. It is known as the "Roller Coaster Capital of the World," which is very accurate as my whole family agrees they had the best coasters we'd been on.
